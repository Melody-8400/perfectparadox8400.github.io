About Us

We are Perfect Paradox Team 8400, located in Cedar City, Utah. We are so excited to have been able to learn and grow this season. This year, we have more than doubled our previous numbers by recruiting 10 new team members. We have impacted over 1,880 people, gained 260+ outreach hours, and 171+ mentor hours. We have also constructed a robot that we are very proud of, and hope to be able to improve it throughout the season.
